About

Jieting (Tina) Chen is a Chinese UX/UI designer. After graduating from Royal College of Art in London, she flew over the pond to take a bite of the Big Apple.

She loves problem solving and crafting awesome user experiences @BarkBox. Her currently side project is Tiny Travel Journal.

When not working, she likes to foster dogs, travel and take photos.
