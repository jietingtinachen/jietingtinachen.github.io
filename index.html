About

Nice to see you here.

I’m currently working at Ro a digital health care company. I work with a cross-functional team to reimagine the future of health care and build superior experiences for our healthcare providers and patients.

Before that, I worked at Bark, a dog-centric company. Where I designed and build products, experiences to make dogs and their human happy.

During my time there, I helped launch a couple of things from the ground up: BarkShop, an online store for dog parents. Super Chewer, a subscription box for big dogs who love to chew. BarkCare, an on-demand in-home vet service for dogs. Crafted a few apps: BarkCam, a camera app specifically designed for dogs. BarkPost app for all fun content about dogs. BarkBuddy, discover and adopt rescued dogs.

Outside work, I love traveling and spending time in nature. Currently, I am building a camper van so I can do more camping and rock climbing.
