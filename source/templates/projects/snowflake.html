Snowflake

A modular system for various indoor usages

Snowflake was inspired by nature and the way snowflake grows and interact within a group.The modularity and customization allow various compositions. It could be a fruit plate, a chandelier or a sculpture in different in door environment.

The full scale prototype has been exhibited during London Design Festival 2010.
