Medusa light

Personal lighting solution for space hotel

A serious comforting personal lamp that can be used to create a visual focal point and provide task lighting. Lamps can be grouped together to illuminate shared space.

AThis project was a collaboration with Alicia Tam and Jessi Baker. Physical models were exhibited during London Design Festival 2010.
