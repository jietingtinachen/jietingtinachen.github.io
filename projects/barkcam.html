BarkCam

A camera app to make your pup next star!

Everytime you try to take a photo of your dogs, they just won't look at the camera. With BarkCam, you can choose a sound from door bell to postman knocking on the door to make sure your dogs look at the camera with their cutest face!

You can also choose dog approved filters, stickers, speech bubble to craft the photo.
