Lemonaid

Make sweet lemonade

After ream five to seven lemons, the reamer will dissolve.

The wood handle and then be used to stir and make tasty lemonade. Lemonaid is a lemon juicer made from sugar. The project was a collaboration with Alicia Tam and Kunal Nandi.

Lemon+Aid was a finalist in the 2011 What’s Bubbling Kitchen Tools competition.It was also shortlisted for the 2010 Oneoff Food Design competition.
