BarkCam

UX / UI / Art direction

BarkCam is a fun app to take photos of your dogs. The problem was that every time you try to take a photo of them, they just won't look at the camera. With BarkCam, you can choose any sound from a grumpy cat to a dinosaur roar to make sure your dogs look at the camera with their cutest faces!

After that, you can also choose all sorts of filters, stickers, speech bubble to decorate the photos.
