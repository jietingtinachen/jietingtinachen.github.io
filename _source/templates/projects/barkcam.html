BarkCam

UX / UI / Art direction

BarkCam is a fun app to take photos of your dogs.

Let's face it, how many times have you try to take a photo of them, the moment you press the button - they are looking at somewhere else? But not with BarkCam. You can choose any sound from a grumpy cat to a crinkly bag to make sure your dogs look at the camera with their cutest faces!

After that, you can also choose all sorts of filters, stickers, speech bubble to decorate the photos.
