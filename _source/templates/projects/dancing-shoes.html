Dancing Shoes

Make music with your dance

The dBn is technology piece that change your step into a instrument.

A pair of shoes fitted with sensors and a micro-controller which are then hardwired into a mobile phone. A granular audio synthesizer and sequencer software is loaded onto the handheld device.

The performer can tune their shoes for a short duration by moving their feet in different directions. Each movement creates a unique sound. Once the tuning session has completed the users can move their feet in any way to generate customized drum and bass beats.

The thick, colorful cables running from the shoes to the device bring out a retro fashion to the performer.
